How does this work?

The Premise

This is a silly little website to help you figure out what type you (or your cat) are. It uses PokeAPI to get random PokeDex descriptions, which you can vote how much they relate. After voting for a while, or after 80 questions, the types will be ranked in order of how much they match your answers.

How is this scored

Scores for types are determined as a percentage range from 0.00% to 100.00%. A score of 100.00% means that all your answers strongly agreed with each example of that type. A score of 0.00% means that all your answers strongly disagreed with each example of that type. Each type starts out with a score of 50.00% (Neutral), and is influenced from there by your answers.

Specifics

If you want to know about the specifics, each score is calculated by taking the sum of your answers on a type, dividing it by the total number of answers on that type, and mapping it to a percentage.

For example:
On my first question, I vote "strongly agree" on a grass type question (+2). My answer sum for grass goes from 0 to 2. My total answers for grass type goes from 0 to 1. My score for grass type can be calculated as 2 / 1 = 2, or 100% on the scale from -2 to 2. If I then vote for another grass type, but only "mostly agree" (+1), this changes:
My answer sum for grass type goes from 2 to 3. My total answers for grass type goes from 1 to 2. My score will then be 3 / 2 = 1.5, or 87.5% on the scale from -2 to 2.
